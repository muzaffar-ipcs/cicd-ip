Hello I'm listening ...

Snapcall is the little green button that answers everything. All you need to do is place it at the bottom of a site's page in order to provide efficient and human-powered customer service. Easy to set up and quick to use, it connects users and agents by placing people at the heart of the customer service experience.

D'NOUR Online

Careful from start to finish.

Our challenge was using the hyperlink as a creative starting point and letting our imagination free to incorporate the concept throughout the site. The design of Snapcall is based on the notion of exchange, a connection between two people, the transmission of words and advice, the flow of information sent and received. The green used signifies commitment, validation, and trust. Daring colors and a real-life aesthetic made the final proposal unique and lasting.

Mix & Match.

Dare to create surprising mixtures by combining a shade of green with a sparkling blue and a bubbly yellow. Soften the visual concept by playing with rounded, elegant, and undulating patterns. Give character and presence to the content by using bold typography with square proportions. These were all bold challenges that our creative team was happy to take on.
